Discussion

This probe describes information about the solar day at the device's current location.

Explanation of probe values:

  • SUNSET and SUNRISE: Timestamps (in milliseconds) when the sun will rise and set.
  • IS_DAY: true if the sun is currently up, false otherwise.
  • DAY_DURATION: Duration of the solar day (in milliseconds).
  • LONGITUDE and LATITUDE: Location coordinates used in the calculations.
  • SUNRISE_DISTANCE and SUNSET_DISTANCE: Temporal distance distance from solar events. A positive value indicates that the day's event is in the past, while a negative value indicates that the event is in the future.
