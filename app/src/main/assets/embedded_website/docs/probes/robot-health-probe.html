Discussion

The Purple Robot Health probe is primarily intended for diagnostic purposes to determine how well Purple Robot is running in its current context.

This probe reports on free space available on the device, the amount of data awaiting transmission, and the cumulative runtime of both the app and device.

In order to track changes in configurations, the probe may be configured to echo its current settings using JSON or Scheme representations by setting the config_probe_robot_json_config and config_probe_robot_scheme_config parameters to true.

To adjust the amount of time between health reports, adjust the frequency parameter for your context. Values of this parameter are expressed in milliseconds between reports, so a setting of 60000 will configure the device to generate a health report once every sixty seconds.
